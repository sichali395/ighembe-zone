About Ighembe Educational Zone

Ighembe Educational Zone is responsible for overseeing and coordinating primary education across 15 schools in the region. Established to ensure quality education delivery, our zone works tirelessly to maintain high educational standards and provide equal learning opportunities for all children.

Our mission is to ensure that every child receives a quality education in a supportive and nurturing environment. We work closely with headteachers, teachers, parents, and the community to maintain high educational standards and continuously improve our teaching methodologies.

Our Mission

To provide quality primary education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values necessary for personal development and contribution to society.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational zone that produces well-rounded, competent, and responsible citizens through excellence in primary education.
